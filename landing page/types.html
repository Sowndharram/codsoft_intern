Types of Pistachios

Turkish Pistachios

Known for their small size, rich flavor, and vibrant green color, Turkish pistachios are a popular choice for their distinct taste and crunchy texture. They are often used in traditional desserts like baklava.

Iranian Pistachios

Larger and slightly more subtly flavored than Turkish pistachios, Iranian pistachios have a buttery taste and are renowned for their high quality. They are a staple in Middle Eastern cuisine.

American Pistachios

Grown primarily in California, American pistachios are known for their larger size and slight sweetness. They are perfect for snacking and are often used in a variety of culinary applications.

Chinese Pistachios

Chinese pistachios are often characterized by their vibrant color and mild flavor. They are usually enjoyed as a snack and are sometimes flavored with spices for added taste.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your pistachios organic?

Yes, all our pistachios are 100% organic, grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. We believe in providing the purest and healthiest nuts.

How should I store pistachios?

Store pistachios in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. For extended freshness, you can refrigerate or freeze them, which will keep them fresh for up to a year.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination. Contact us for more details on international shipping options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For bulk orders, we also accept purchase orders and offer flexible payment terms.
